Park Chanyeol was born in Seoul, South Korea on November 27, 1992. He attended Hyundai Chungun High School in Dong-gu, Ulsan. When he was 16 years old, he studied at a private acting institute. He played as a drummer, with a band formed at his school, and won second place in a television modeling contest. Chanyeol was entered into SM Entertainment during the SM Casting System in 2008. Along with other EXO members, Suho and Kai, he made a brief appearance in TVXQ's MV entitled "HaHaHa Song" in 2008. He also appeared in Girls' Generation's MV entitled "Genie" in Japanese which was released in August 2010. He was the last member to be introduced on February 23, 2012.
